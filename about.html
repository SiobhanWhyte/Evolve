THE EVOLVE COMMUNITY

Established in 2017 and now into our 5th year in business we pride ourselves on the quality of our coaching. We offer 27 classes each week for Adults and 9 gymnastics classes for kids. Every class that we do can be tailored and altered to suit any individual with all movements and alternatives and progressions demonstrated by our qualified team of coaches.
We cap all of our classes at the gym to guarantee that everyone feels comfortable and has plenty of space and this results in a better delivery of the class and the knowledge that you will always be looked after and monitored by our coaches. Fun, Varied and Challenging, you are guaranteed a different workout every time you come to the gym so get in touch today and let us look after you and help you get fitter, stronger and healthier.

Morgan
Head Coach and Owner

Morgan was a highly competitive sports player and played to a high standard in basketball, football, and GAA. Morgan qualified as a fitness professional in 2014. From here he worked locally for 2 years before deciding to go out on his own. Morgan set up home help fitness and nutrition blocks and he set up sky blue fitness on his own out in Allenwood. Morgan also worked coaching Gymnastics in Panthers Academy for 18 months. In June 2017 Morgan got keys to his own unit here in Newbridge and set up Evolve Strength and Fitness.
