What Classes are available for Adults?

We offer 2 types of classes for adults. Bootcamp Strength and Conditioning. Bootcamp classes are hugely popular and will guarantee an overall improvement in both strength and fitness levels.

How long are the classes for?

All classes are done within One hour incorporating warm-up, demonstration, workout, and cool down.

What to expect from our Bootcamp class at Evolve?

The combination of movements from weighted to bodyweight and the huge variety in structure and adaptation changing work and rest times and intensity levels does help improve all aspects of your health and fitness. All of this is done in a fun and challenging way and we never do the same workout twice.

What to expect from our Strength and conditioning class at Evolve?

We like to offer a progression for people. These classes are more advanced as we start to include structured programming around strength training and include more complex movements with barbells and more intense conditioning sessions. These classes are 1 hour and 15 mins long and are not a drop-in option. The benefits from these classes will only come with long-term consistency and doing an odd class every now again is pointless. We have our own unique structure to these classes and we have an amazing and dedicated strength crew who train with us at the gym and who have been going from strength to strength.
